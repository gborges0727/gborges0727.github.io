About Me

I'm currently an undergraduate senior at UConn pursuing a dual degree in both Computer Science and English. It's a weird combo, but my philosophy was always that I had to pursue what I love. I had always loved English in high school, taking courses such as creative writing and contemporary global literature, so continuing it as a degree in college only seemed natural. I also had a deep love for computers and software which is what made me pursue a computer science minor. I realized I could finish the full degree with one extra year so I decided to go for it. When I'm not at school, I love to spend time with my friends and family. Some of my hobbies include: programming, chess and videogames.
